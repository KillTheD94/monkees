Last Train To Clarksville

Last Train to Clarksville was the Monkees debut song released right at the beginning in 1966. The story of the song features Clarksvill Tennessee, where the 101st US Airbourne were stationed. The song features a couple, where the man may be going to Vietnam to fight the Vietnam war.

Daydream Believer

Daydream Believer was the last US number one for The Monkees, topping the charts for 4 weeks, and reached number 5 in the UK charts. The song provides a view of modern suburban life.

I'm A Believer

The Monkees covered I'm A Believer after Neil Diamond had written it. The song was released before the end of 1966 and saw it top the charts on 31st December of that year, it would remain at the top of the charts for seven weeks.

I'm Not Your Stepping Stone

I'm Not Your Stepping Stone was also released in 1966 and was the bands first B-side to rerach the charts, this is one the The Monkees most successful songs, and has been covered by notable punk band, The Sex Pistols
